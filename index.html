Quick Brief — What is this and where are we?
We are building an offline data factory for MAL — the domain language used by the ALM platform. The factory takes 56 manually written gold seed examples, expands them into 336 balanced variants using Jinja2 templates, then uses GPT-4o to synthesize rich training records, routes everything through a 5-stage quality gate, and stores validated results in a browsable training registry. All 5 pipeline stages are now live with a full React/Vite/TypeScript frontend dashboard. See Section 7b for the full user flow — every page, every button, and what each step produces. See Section 7d for the complete page-by-page walkthrough.
